# Roadmap

## Overview

Apokyfros has massive goals for the future. I aspire to create the best possible MMORPG using only python. So in the following, I include what I imagine my game is to become and along with this, the specific phases I want the game to enter as development continues.

## Vision

Apokyfros is a vast, mystical world where every choice shapes destiny. Players will pledge loyalty to one of two ancient nemesis clans — the radiant Helios or the shadowed Selene — each locked in an eternal struggle for supremacy. The game will feature dozens of diverse zones filled with formidable monsters and rare, legendary resources, essential for crafting powerful weapons and unlocking extraordinary magic. Through exploration, combat, trading, and mastery of strategy, players will rise from humble warriors to legendary heroes, leaving their mark on a living, evolving world.
